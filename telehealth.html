The Process

How Telehealth Works ⭐

Your journey to better health in 5 simple, secure, and seamless steps.

1

Register Account

Create your secure health profile in seconds. Add your basic details and medical history to get personalized care tailored for you. Rest assured that your data is heavily encrypted under strict healthcare compliance laws. You have complete control over your profile and can update medical records anytime.

2

Book Appointment

Browse through our certified specialists, pick a convenient time slot, and schedule your online consultation instantly. Filter doctors by their ratings, experience, and specific medical expertise. You will receive instant SMS and email confirmations along with a pre-consultation checklist.

3

Connect With Doctor

Join the high-definition, fully encrypted video call. Discuss your symptoms and get a comprehensive medical evaluation. Share your past medical reports directly through the secure chat during the live session. The platform automatically adjusts video quality based on your internet connection for zero interruptions.

4

Receive Prescription

Get a digital prescription immediately after your call. You can download it or forward it directly to your local pharmacy. Our integrated pharmacy partners can also deliver the prescribed medicines straight to your doorstep within hours. You can access your complete prescription history from the dashboard whenever needed.

5

Track Recovery

Stay connected with follow-up reminders, track your vital signs, and access 24/7 support until you are fully recovered. Log your daily symptoms and get AI-driven health insights to speed up your recovery journey. Your assigned specialist will be notified instantly if any critical alerts are detected in your health logs.
