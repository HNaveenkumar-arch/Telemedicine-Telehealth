Got Questions?
We've Got Answers

Find out everything you need to know about our secure telemedicine services and health guidelines.

How do online consultations work?

Simply book an appointment through our platform, and you will receive a secure video link via email and SMS. At the scheduled time, click the link to join a private video call with your doctor from the comfort of your home. You can use your smartphone, tablet, or computer. If your connection drops, our system allows you to easily reconnect without losing your session time.

Are prescriptions legally valid?

Yes, absolutely. Our certified doctors provide digital prescriptions that are legally valid and recognized by health authorities. These prescriptions contain a secure digital signature and can be used to purchase medications at any local pharmacy or ordered directly through our partnered online medicine delivery services for doorstep drop-off.

Is my medical data secure?

We take your privacy seriously. All your medical records, consultation history, video streams, and personal data are encrypted with AES-256 military-grade security. Our platform strictly complies with global healthcare privacy standards (HIPAA), ensuring that only you and your assigned healthcare provider have access to your sensitive health information.

Can I choose my preferred doctor?

Yes! You can browse through our extensive directory of specialized and verified doctors. You can filter them by specialty, experience, language spoken, and patient ratings. Once you find a doctor you feel comfortable with, you can select them for your current consultation and even save them as your primary care physician for future visits.

What if I need physical tests or lab work?

If your doctor determines that physical tests, blood work, or X-rays are necessary, they will provide a digital referral through the app. You can take this referral to any of our partnered diagnostic centers or a local lab of your choice. Once your test results are ready, you can upload them directly into the app for your doctor to review during a follow-up session.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept a wide range of major health insurance plans. During the booking process, you can enter your insurance details to automatically check for coverage eligibility and copay requirements. We also provide detailed digital invoices and claim forms that you can submit to your insurance provider for reimbursement if you are using an out-of-network plan.
